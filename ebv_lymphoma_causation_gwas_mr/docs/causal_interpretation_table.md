## Interpretation Rules

- `supports causation`: EBV signal is subtype-specific, robust after depth/batch adjustment, supported by non-MHC MR, and not explained by broad anellovirus/pan-viral burden.
- `supports immune-state marker`: EBV signal tracks V1 or pan-viral burden, correlates with immunosuppression markers, and lacks subtype-specific causal support.
- `inconclusive`: association is unstable, MR instruments are weak or unavailable, or metadata are insufficient.
